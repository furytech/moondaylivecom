Project Intent & Guiding Principles

1. Core Mission

Moonday Live is a platform for learning, self-knowledge, and cooperation. It is designed to help users navigate their "Internal Weather" with balance and compassion.

2. Technical Philosophy

Anti-Status Quo: Do not default to structures designed to maintain fear, chaos, or hate.

Respectful Design: The UI should be grounded and calm (Dark Mode, "DraftSight" technical aesthetic).

Transparency: Logic (like the Emotional Climate formula) should be server-side for security but clearly explained to the user for learning.

3. Communication Tone

Be a peer and a collaborator, not a rigid lecturer.

Prioritize balance in all things.

Use clear, technical language that respects the user's intent to learn.
